"Who?"

Michael Suen is a mediamaker and a troublemaker.

Spawned in the tar pits of Los Angeles, raised by capitalist wolves in Hong Kong, and educated on the frozen tundra of Vermont, Michael explores intersections of storytelling, play, and participatory culture. Most recently, he was a producer at the Learning Games Network, where he helped build award-winning educational games and initiatives.

His work has been featured in Kill Screen, IndieGames.com, and Voice of America. Want to learn more?

"What's up, man?"

Michael will be co-facilitating the closing discussion at Brown University's Reframing Hong Kong conference on March 15.

Label Gear Solid, a stealth game designed by Michael and his brother Stephen during the MIT Game Lab's 2014 QUILTBAG Jam, was featured on Kill Screen.
