Spawned in the tar pits of Los Angeles, raised by capitalist wolves in Hong Kong, and educated on the ice planet of Vermont, Michael is a designer, illustrator, and writer who straddles the intersections of meaningful design, storytelling, and participation. He cut his teeth as a digital producer for Learning Games Network, where he helped build award-winning educational projects and amplify them for broader audiences.

Michael's work has been featured in Kill Screen, IndieGames.com, and Voice of America. He has presented on transmedia storytelling, games for learning, and social media at places like MIT, Bryn Mawr College, Brown University, and HASTAC. Michael lives in Cambridge, MA with his PlayStation.
