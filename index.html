I'm a designer, illustrator, and writer. My work emphasizes user-friendly experiences that blur the line between work and play. I work with startups, small businesses, and creative entrepreneurs to build web products, games, and brands that audiences love to use.

Projects I've worked on have been featured in Wired, NPR, and Voice of America. In my former life, I helped produce award-winning games and game-based learning initiatives at the MIT spin-off  Learning Games Network.

Spawned in the tar pits of L.A., raised by capitalist wolves in Hong Kong, and educated on the ice planet of Vermont, I now live in Cambridge, MA with my PlayStation 3.
